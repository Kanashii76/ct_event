How Moovie! Was Born

A quirky idea, some dairy farmers, and a whole lot of moo-tivation.

The Story

In the summer of 2025, our founder Brie was working late at a tech startup when she got a call from her cousin who owns a working dairy farm. He was frustrated: his cows had gotten loose during a movie night he was hosting, and somehow—inexplicably—they made the whole thing better. The herd wandered through the viewing area, the movie-goers laughed harder than they had in years, and Brie realized something magical was happening.

Two weeks later, Brie quit her job, moved to the countryside, and launched Moovie!—a series of curated movie events hosted on an actual working farm, where the real stars aren't always on screen. What started as a one-off experiment has turned into a thriving community of movie lovers, cow enthusiasts, and people who just want to experience something completely different.

Today, Moovie! hosts events every weekend during the season, and our herd has grown from 4 cows to 12 permanent members—each with their own personality and fan following.

Our Mission

Create Joy

We believe entertainment should be surprising, memorable, and a little bit absurd. We bring people together through movies and cows—in any order.

Celebrate the Unexpected

The best moments in life are unplanned. Our events embrace the chaos, the mooing during emotional scenes, the random cow photo bombs.

Support Local

100% of Moovie! events are hosted on working farms. We partner with local dairy farmers and reinvest proceeds back into agriculture education.

Our Values

Authenticity

We're not pretending this is normal. A herd of cows at a movie night is weird, and that's the whole point.

Community Over Profit

Moovie! exists to bring people together, not to milk them for money. (Okay, maybe one cow pun.)

Sustainability

Every event supports the working farm it's hosted on. We believe in keeping agriculture alive and accessible to everyone.

Fun First

If it's not fun—for our team, our cows, and our attendees—we don't do it. Life's too short for boring movie nights.

Our Timeline

Summer 2025

The Great Cow Escape. Brie gets the idea after her cousin's farm mishap. First experimental event.

Fall 2025

Moovie! launches officially. Monthly events fill up within 48 hours. Local press starts calling.

Winter 2025

Expanded to bi-weekly events. Our herd grows from 4 to 8 cows. Bessie becomes a local celebrity.

Spring 2026

Year-round operations begin. We hit 12 cows and partner with 3 additional farms in the region.

Now

Moovie! is a thriving community with thousands of attendees, a waitlist a mile long, and dreams of expanding nationwide.
